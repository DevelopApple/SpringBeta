Installation

  1. Turn off TouchID and password in Settings
  2. Download the Spring.ipa file and Cydia Impactor
  3. Open Cydia Impactor and connect your device
  4. Drag the Spring.ipa file onto the Cydia Impactor window
  5. Enter your Apple ID (email and password)
  6. Open Settings → General → Profiles & Device Management
  7. Trust the new certificate → open Spring
  8. Tap 'Jailbreak' and enjoy!
